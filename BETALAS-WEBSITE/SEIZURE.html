SEIZURE FIRST AID

WHAT IS SEIZURE ?

A seizure is a sudden surge of abnormal electrical activity in the brain. It can cause changes in movement, behavior, feelings, or consciousness. Seizures can look very different depending on the type — from full-body convulsions to brief staring spells.

Seizure first aid is the immediate care and actions you take to help someone who is having a seizure. The goal is to keep the person safe, prevent injury, and provide supportive care until the seizure ends or medical help arrives.

MORE INFORMATION

We have included a video for you to watch in order to gain a better understanding of how to do “Seizure First Aid.” and more information about Seizure.

WHEN CAN I USE SEIZURE FIRST AID?

You should use seizure first aid as soon as you witness someone having a seizure. Recognizing the signs early and responding immediately helps keep the person safe and can prevent serious injury.
